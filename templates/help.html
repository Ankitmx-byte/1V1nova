Getting Started

What is CodeBattle?

CodeBattle is a platform where programmers can improve their coding skills by solving challenges, competing in real-time battles, and learning from comprehensive tutorials and courses. Our platform is designed to help coders of all levels enhance their problem-solving abilities and prepare for technical interviews.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the homepage. You can sign up using your email address, or through Google, GitHub, or Facebook authentication. After signing up, you'll need to verify your email address to activate your account.

Is CodeBattle free to use?

CodeBattle offers both free and premium features. The free tier gives you access to a limited number of challenges, battles, and learning resources. Premium membership unlocks all challenges, advanced analytics, premium courses, and more. You can view our pricing plans on the Pricing page.

What programming languages are supported?

CodeBattle currently supports JavaScript, Python, Java, C++, C#, Ruby, Go, Swift, Kotlin, and TypeScript. We're constantly adding support for more languages based on user feedback and demand.
